Types of Mental Disorders

Anxiety

Pepole with anxiety disorders respond to certain object or situations with fear as well as with physcical signs of anxiety.


Depression

A mental health disorder characterised by persistently depressed mood or loss of interest in activities, causing significant impairment in daily life.

Eating Disorder

Eating disorders are a type of serious mental health condition characterized by severe disturbances in eating behaviors.

Common symptoms of Mental Disorders

Following are some of the common symptoms observed among mentally disturbed people

Feelings of sadness, tearfulness, emptiness
or hopelessness

Loss of interest or pleasure in most or all
normal activities, such as hobbies or sports

Sleep disturbances, including insomnia
or sleeping too much

Tiredness and lack of energy, so even small tasks take extra effort

Frequent or recurrent thoughts of death,
suicidal thoughts, suicide attempts or suicide

Treatments On Mental Issues

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy


In general, treatment can involve medications, talk therapy, exposure therapy, support groups, and self-help. It is the most effective form of psychotherapy for anxiety disorders.

Psychodynamic Therapy


Psychodynamic therapy is an approach that involves facilitation a deeper understanding of one's emotions and other mental processes. It works to help people gain greater insight into how they feel and think.

Doctor's Advice


It is sensible to take psychiatric doctor's help, if someone feels mentally unstable for longer periods of time. Doctor's advice can result in proper medication and good improvement of a person.
